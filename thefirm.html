The Firm

S. C. AJMERA & CO. is a reputable partnership firm established on 25 May 1980, with its head office located in Thane and branch offices in Udaipur, Mumbai, Indore, and Sambalpur. The firm is registered under Registration No. 002908C and is empaneled with the Central Government, RBI, and CAG, providing audit services to entities with turnover or deposits exceeding 500 crores. It holds PAN No. AAQFS9420E and GST Registration No. 08AAQFS9420E1ZW, and its chartered accountants are certified by ICAI. The firm is committed to providing high-quality audit and financial consultancy services to diverse sectors including banking, insurance, infrastructure, education, and cooperative societies.

With over four decades of experience, S. C. AJMERA & CO. specializes in statutory audits, tax audits, system audits, concurrent audits, internal financial control audits, and taxation consultancy. The firm has successfully conducted audits for prominent organizations such as the State Bank of Bikaner & Jaipur, Dena Bank, ONGC, BSNL, Power System Operation Corporation, and many others. It is also actively engaged in audits for universities, hospitals, non-governmental organizations, and cooperative societies across India.

The team is led by highly experienced partners, including CA Suresh C. Ajmera, CA Arun Sarupria, CA Satish Shakkya, CA Madan Lal Jain, CA K. K. Agrawal, CA Ashok Kumar Gang, CA Pritam Sharma, and CA Sheetal Choudhary. Many of the partners are accredited with FCA and DISA qualifications and have attended specialized training in concurrent audit and information systems auditing. Their leadership and technical expertise enable the firm to offer tailor-made solutions that meet regulatory requirements and business goals.

Over the years, the firm has built a strong reputation for integrity, professionalism, and excellence. It emphasizes ethical conduct and team collaboration, ensuring that clients receive timely and precise guidance on audit and taxation matters. The firm has conducted audits for major PSU companies, banks, insurance providers, manufacturing units, infrastructure projects, and educational institutions, consistently helping organizations maintain compliance and strengthen financial management.

Committed to fostering long-term client relationships, S. C. AJMERA & CO. continues to expand its services while adapting to emerging technologies and global standards. The firm remains a trusted advisor, offering comprehensive financial audits, tax compliance, internal controls, valuation, liquidation services, and arbitration support tailored to the needs of various sectors.
